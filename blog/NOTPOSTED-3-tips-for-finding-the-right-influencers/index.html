3 Tips to Find the Right Influence Marketers

Posted on May 31, 2018

Influencer Marketing and your Business

Influencer marketing is not something new, only that businesses are waking up to its potential now. It is an efficient way of leveraging other people’s following or communities to increase exposure for your products and services. But with so many influencers spread across the various platforms, it should be understood that not every influencer will be ideal for your business. To be successful in influencer marketing, you must know how to locate their right influencers, and here are a few factors you should consider.

The Niche

Your perfect influencer should be in the same niche as you are. It is how you will be assured of putting your products or services in front of people who are genuinely passionate about them. If you choose to work with influencers in different niches because they are affordable or have huge followings, you may have some success, but the campaign may not be as effective as it would have been if you had chosen an influencer in your niche.

The Rate of Engagement of the Fans

In as much as you would like to work with influencer with huge fan bases, they will be useless if they don’t have any engagement. With the available technology, some influencers have grown their following with bots, and so they are don’t have real people following them. These are the kinds of influencers you don’t want to work with. Examine influencer’s followers and check on their level of engagement to determine that they are real people. Only then should you go ahead and work with them.

The Size of the Following

The size of the following should be considered when looking into engagement. You need to work with an influencer with a decent following and a sizeable rate of engagement. Influencers with the very small following might not be very useful even if they had a lot of engagement.
